# TV image assets

Put your TV images in this folder.

Current filenames used by the app:

- `tv on.png` - used when the TV is powered on
- `tv off.png` - used when the TV is powered off

Recommended:

- Use PNG or JPG.
- Keep both images the same size.
- The current images are full scene renders at `1672x941`.
- The app places the video over the TV screen and invisible click zones over the real TV buttons.
- If you use another file type, update the paths in `app/components/TVFrame.tsx`.
